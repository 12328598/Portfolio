What is Social media marketing

Social media marketing (also known as digital marketing and e-marketing) is the use of social media—the platforms on which users build social networks and share information—to build a company's brand, increase sales, and drive website traffic..

Social media strategy planning starts with your goals. Whether you want to expand your team, build a larger following or a more active community, taking the time to define your social goals is the first step to reaching them. Either way, your goals will define your social media marketing strategy and how much time and energy you’ll need to dedicate to your campaigns..
